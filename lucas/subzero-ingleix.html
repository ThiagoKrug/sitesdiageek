Mortal Kombat story

This franchise is being product since 1992 and a lof of stories being told. But, in general, always based in the same premise. Extremely skilled warriors are invited to participate in a tournament which must battle each other and prove themselves as The Grand Champion of Mortal Kombat. The main characters of the plot are the friends Liu Kang and Kung Lao. Liu Kang became the tournament's grand champion, who's being trained by Rayde, God of Thunder.

Character story

In the Universe, there are 2 Sub-Zeros, the brother Bi-Han and Kuai Liang, born in the Lin Kuei clan. Bi-Han, the eldest brother, was the original Sub-Zero, Adopting the elegant blue tunic. However, he ended up being assassinated by his nemesis, Scorpion. The younger brother, after Bi-Han's death, chose Bi-Han style,, becoming the new Sub-Zero, swearing to get revenge on Scorpion.
